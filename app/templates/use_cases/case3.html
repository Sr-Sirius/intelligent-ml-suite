Healthcare Risk Prediction

Problem Description

Healthcare systems aim to identify patients at risk of developing serious conditions early. Predicting health risks allows for preventive care and better treatment outcomes.

Type of Machine Learning

This is a supervised classification problem, where the model predicts whether a patient is at high or low risk.

Input Data

The model uses patient data such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and medical history.

Expected Output

The system outputs a classification indicating whether the patient is at high or low risk of developing a condition.

Real-World Relevance

Hospitals use predictive models to improve early diagnosis and optimize treatment plans, reducing healthcare costs and improving patient outcomes.
