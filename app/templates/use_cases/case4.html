Car Price and Risk Analysis

Problem Description

In the automotive industry, determining the value or risk associated with a vehicle is essential for buyers, sellers, and insurers. Accurately predicting car prices or assessing risk factors helps improve decision-making and market transparency.

Type of Machine Learning

This use case can involve:

  • Regression →to predict car prices
  • Classification →to determine risk level (e.g., high-risk vs low-risk vehicle)

Input Data

Relevant features include:

  • Car age
  • Mileage
  • Engine size
  • Brand and model
  • Maintenance history

These variables influence both price and risk.

Expected Output

Depending on the model:

  • Predicted car price (regression)
  • Risk category(classification)

Real-World Relevance

Car dealerships, insurance companies, and online marketplaces use machine learning to estimate vehicle value and assess risk. This improves pricing accuracy and helps customers make better purchasing decisions.
