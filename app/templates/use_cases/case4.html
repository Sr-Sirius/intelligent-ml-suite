Car Price and Risk Analysis

Problem Description

Determining the value and risk of a vehicle is essential for buyers, sellers, and insurers. Machine learning helps predict car prices and assess risk factors.

Type of Machine Learning

This use case can involve regression for price prediction and classification for risk assessment.

Input Data

Features include car age, mileage, engine size, brand, and maintenance history.

Expected Output

The system predicts the car price or classifies the vehicle into a risk category.

Real-World Relevance

Car dealerships and insurance companies use these models to determine pricing and assess vehicle risks.
